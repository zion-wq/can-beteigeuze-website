The Villa

A private sea-view villa above Cala Vadella

3 bedrooms · 2 bathrooms · terrace · BBQ · sea views · 10 min to Cala Vadella

About the villa

A home on the Ibiza coast

Can Beteigeuze is a private villa in the style of traditional Ibiza architecture — warm, comfortable and unpretentious. A genuine home where everything feels right from the moment you arrive. The living room opens onto the terrace, from where the sea is always in view.

The villa sleeps up to 6 guests across 3 bedrooms. Whether you come as a family, couple, or small group of friends — there is space, privacy and comfort for everyone.

3 bedrooms  ·  2 bathrooms  ·  Max 6 guests  ·  10 min walk to Cala Vadella  ·  Sea views from terrace & living room  ·  Private parking  ·  Cleaning included

Room by room

Rooms & terrace

Terrace & outdoor living

The heart of the villa. A covered terrace with a large dining table seats everyone comfortably. The lounge area with its built-in sofa faces the sea. The BBQ is always ready. This is where every evening ends.

The sea view from here is unobstructed — no buildings, no other terraces. Just the Mediterranean and the sky.

Covered terrace · outdoor dining · lounge · BBQ · outdoor shower

Living room

Large, bright and comfortable. The living room connects directly to the terrace through wide glass doors, so the sea is never far. Rattan chairs, a comfortable sofa, bookshelves — a space to be in, not just pass through.

TV, Sonos sound system and fast WiFi are available.

Kitchen

Fully equipped for proper cooking: large fridge, oven, hob, dishwasher, coffee machine and all the cookware you need. Perfect for a relaxed family dinner or a more elaborate evening meal.

Master bedroom

The largest bedroom, with its own en-suite bathroom. Double bed and air conditioning. Light and quiet, with a view of the garden and trees.

En-suite bathroom · Double bed (160×200 cm) · Air conditioning

Bedroom 2

A generously sized room with great natural light — large enough to comfortably hold three beds. Shares the main bathroom just down the hall.

3 beds · Air conditioning

Bedroom 3

Two single beds, making it ideal for children or a pair of friends. Light room with garden view and access to the main bathroom.

2 single beds (90×200 cm) · Air conditioning

Bathrooms

The main bathroom is a highlight of the villa: wooden beams, natural stone details, a freestanding bath by the window and a rain shower. The en-suite bathroom off the master bedroom has a walk-in shower.

2 bathrooms total · bathtub · 2 showers · underfloor heating

Who stays at Can Beteigeuze

A place to settle in and slow down

Families, couples and small groups of friends who want privacy, sea views and their own pace on the southwest coast of Ibiza. Three bedrooms, space for six, and Cala Vadella beach ten minutes through the pine trees.

Most guests find a rhythm quickly. Mornings on the terrace with the sea in front of you. Afternoons at the beach. Evenings around the BBQ with the lights of the coast below and no reason to be anywhere else.

A very well-kept villa, an extraordinary view and personal service from the moment you arrive. The southwest coast opens onto some of the best beaches and restaurants on the island — everything at an easy distance.
