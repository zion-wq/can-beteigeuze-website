Location & beach

About 10 minutes on foot, following a path through the pine trees. The walk is pleasant and shaded. For families with young children or guests who prefer not to walk, the beach is also reachable by car in 3 minutes (with parking nearby).

More than you could visit in a week. Cala Vadella is on your doorstep — ten minutes on foot. Cala d'Hort, with its straight-on view of Es Vedrà, is five minutes by car. From there the coast opens up: Cala Tarida, Cala Conta, Cala Bassa, Cala Gracioneta, Es Cavallet, Ses Salines. Rocky coves, wide sandy bays, beach clubs, completely empty spots — every mood, every day of the week. The southwest coast of Ibiza is simply one of the best stretches of coastline in the Mediterranean. We'll make sure you find the ones that suit you.

For the beach and a few nearby restaurants you can walk, but for supermarket shopping and exploring the island a car is strongly recommended. There is private parking at the villa. We suggest renting a car at the airport on arrival.

Booking & payment

Simple. You send an enquiry via our booking form or by email. We confirm availability and send you payment details within 24 hours.

Payment is 30% deposit on booking confirmation, and the remaining 70% at least 30 days before arrival.

Vrbo charges guests a service fee of around 8–12% on top of the rental price. When you book directly, there is no platform involved, so no fee. The price we quote is the price you pay. Plus you get direct contact with the owner, faster responses and more flexibility.

In the low season (November – March), electricity is not included in the weekly rate. The villa has underfloor heating throughout, which can be a significant cost in winter. Electricity is billed separately at the end of your stay, based on actual usage. We will always be transparent about the meter reading on arrival and departure.

€600. This is paid before arrival and returned in full within 7 days of departure, provided there is no damage to the villa or its contents. The deposit is a standard precaution — almost all of our guests receive it back in full.

Cancellation more than 30 days before arrival: 50% of the deposit is refunded. Cancellation within 30 days of arrival: the deposit is non-refundable. We recommend travel insurance that covers cancellation costs.

If you have exceptional circumstances, please get in touch — we always try to find a fair solution.

During your stay

Check-in is from 16:00. Check-out is before 10:00. Early check-in or late check-out may be possible on request, depending on availability before and after your stay. Please ask when you book.

Check-in and check-out are handled by our local concierge team, who will welcome you at the villa and take care of the handover. They are also available throughout your stay should you have any questions or need anything.

Unfortunately we are not able to accommodate pets at the villa.

Smoking is not permitted inside the villa. Outside on the terrace, smoking is allowed.
