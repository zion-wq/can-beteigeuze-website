The experience

A day at Can Beteigeuze

You wake up as the first light plays across the sea. Coffee outside, in the silence — nothing but the sound of the wind in the pine trees. This is how a day here begins.

After breakfast, you walk to Cala Vadella in ten minutes. A sheltered bay, calm water, a beach bar where the day drifts slowly by. Back in the afternoon, a siesta in the shade.

Towards evening the sky turns orange and red. On the terrace, wine in hand, you watch the sun sink into the sea. Light the BBQ. Dinner outside. Talking late into the night.

The kind of day that reminds you why you came here.

The location

Cala Vadella — the bay locals keep to themselves

Cala Vadella is just around the corner — a sheltered bay with turquoise water, a beach bar, and the atmosphere of an Ibiza that has always been there. Ten minutes on foot.

Around the villa: restaurants, a supermarket and the authentic west coast of Ibiza. Not the party island — but the most beautiful part of it.
