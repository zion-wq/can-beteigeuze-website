Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Who we are

This website belongs to the owners of Can Beteigeuze, a private holiday villa located in Cala Vadella, Ibiza, Spain. You can contact us at info@can-beteigeuze.com.

What data we collect

We collect personal data in the following situations:

Booking enquiry form — When you submit an enquiry via the contact form on this website, we collect your name, email address, phone number (optional) and the details you provide (travel dates, group size, message). This data is used solely to respond to your enquiry and, if you proceed, to manage your booking. We do not share this data with third parties.

Google Analytics — This website uses Google Analytics to understand how visitors use the site (pages visited, time spent, device type). No personally identifiable information is collected. Analytics only activates after you accept cookies via the cookie notice. You can withdraw your consent at any time by clearing your browser cookies.

Legal basis

We process your personal data on the basis of your consent (analytics) and to fulfil a pre-contractual request (booking enquiry). We do not use your data for marketing purposes without your explicit consent.

How long we keep your data

Booking-related correspondence is kept for a maximum of 2 years. Analytics data is retained by Google Analytics per their standard data retention settings.

Your rights

Under the GDPR you have the right to access, correct or request deletion of your personal data. To exercise these rights, please contact us at info@can-beteigeuze.com. We will respond within 30 days.

Third-party services

This website uses the following third-party services: Google Analytics (Google LLC, USA), Google Fonts (Google LLC, USA), and Netlify for hosting (Netlify Inc., USA). Each of these services has its own privacy policy.

Cookies

We use one first-party cookie to remember your cookie preference, and Google Analytics cookies if you have accepted analytics. No tracking or advertising cookies are used.

Questions about this policy? Email us at info@can-beteigeuze.com.
